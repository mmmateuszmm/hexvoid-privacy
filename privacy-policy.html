HEXVOID PRIVACY POLICY & TERMS OF SERVICE 1. Introduction HEXVOID ("the Game", "we", "us", "our") is a mobile arcade game developed as an independent project. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding that data. By downloading or playing HEXVOID, you agree to the practices described in this document. If you do not agree, please do not use the Game. 2. Data We Collect We collect the following categories of information: Account data — nickname (chosen by you), username tag, and optional Google account identifier (UID) if you sign in with Google. Game progress — high scores, XP level, owned skins and items, achievement progress, daily login streak, game settings. Gameplay statistics — number of games played, walls passed, coins collected, play time. Used for leaderboards and achievements only. Device & technical data — crash reports (stack traces, device model, OS version) collected automatically by Firebase Crashlytics to fix bugs. Analytics events — anonymised in-game events (e.g. "game started", "shop opened") collected by Firebase Analytics to understand how the game is used. Advertising data — if you accept personalised ads (GDPR consent), Google AdMob may collect advertising identifiers and usage data. If you decline, only non-personalised ads are shown and no profiling occurs. Country / region — detected from your IP address via a free geolocation service (ip2c.org) solely to display local leaderboards. We do not store your IP address. We do not collect: real name, email address (unless provided by Google Sign-In), phone number, location (GPS), contacts, camera, or microphone data. 3. How We Use Your Data To save and sync your game progress across devices (Firebase Firestore). To display global, weekly, and local leaderboards. To show relevant or non-personalised advertisements (Google AdMob). To fix crashes and improve game stability (Firebase Crashlytics). To understand which game features are popular (Firebase Analytics). We do not sell your data to third parties. We do not use your data for any purpose other than operating and improving HEXVOID. 4. Third-Party Services HEXVOID uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies: Google Firebase (Auth, Firestore, Analytics, Crashlytics) — firebase.google.com/support/privacy Google AdMob — policies.google.com/privacy Google Sign-In — policies.google.com/privacy ip2c.org — free IP-to-country lookup, no personal data stored. 5. Advertising & GDPR Consent On first launch, the Game presents a consent dialog in compliance with the EU General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and Google's advertising policies. Accept — personalised ads shown based on your interests. AdMob may process your advertising identifier. Decline — only non-personalised ads shown. No profiling. The Game remains fully playable. You can change your ad preference at any time in the Game's Settings → Privacy. 6. Data Retention & Deletion Guest data is stored only on your device (localStorage). If you clear the app's storage in Android Settings, all local guest data is permanently deleted. Google account data is stored in Firebase Firestore. To request deletion of your cloud data, contact us at the address below. We will delete your record within 30 days. 7. Children's Privacy HEXVOID is rated for ages 12 and above.
